About West Coast Cycle, Inc

For 30 years we have been a family owned and operated independent Motorcycle dealer and repair shop for all Japanese motorcycles; Kawasaki, Honda, Suzuki and Yamaha and occasionally USA made Harley Davidson.

We specialize in major or minor motorcycle repair of these brands as well as locating and selling used parts and accessories. Large selection of O.E.M. new and used Salvage motorcycle parts on site.

We are the oldest motorcycle repair and salvage parts shop in Tampa. We opened in 1982 and have been serving the Tampa Bay area ever since. Our goal is to make your experience with us a pleasant and affordable one.

We are family owned and operated and started our business in 1982.

We will service and repair all Japanese Motorcycles.

Located in the rear of our shop is our Salvage where you can find thousands of used O.E.M parts. We sell new, original or aftermarket parts and accessories.

We also offer restoration or detail services on your bike to like new condition.

We buy, sell and consign late model Sport Bikes and Cruisers.

Paint work, Insurance Estimates, Towing service and more. We strive to give our customers the best.

Proudly serving two of the worlds fastest motorcycle RECORD HOLDER (BILL WARNER, 311 mph) and Renowned Racing Champion Tony Nicosia.
